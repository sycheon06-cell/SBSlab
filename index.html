Innovating for Sustainable Building Energy

Welcome to the Sustainable Building Systems Lab (SBS Lab) at Hanyang University. We specialize in advanced HVAC systems, building energy optimization, and next-generation dehumidification technologies.

Our Research Publications

About the Lab

Led by Research Assistant Professor Seong-Yong Cheon, our lab is part of the Department of Architectural Engineering at Hanyang University.

We focus on solving critical challenges in building energy efficiency and indoor environmental quality. Our research spans liquid desiccant dehumidifiers, membrane technologies, and dedicated outdoor air systems (DOAS).
